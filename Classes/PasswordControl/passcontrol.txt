  Description:  PassControl is a BTextControl object that masks what is being entered. Just pop it into your code, change a couple of things and it works! (theoretically. your mileage may vary) Competely free and ready to use in you app today. Now with multi-byte character support, a dynamic buffer for the "actual" password storage, a smart SetText(), and other tweaks
